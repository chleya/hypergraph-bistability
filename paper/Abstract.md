# Abstract

We present a unified theory of multistability in capacity-constrained competitive dynamics on hypergraphs. Starting from the one-dimensional order-parameter system that admits at most bistability (asymmetric double-well potential with critical capacity $K_c/N \approx 0.35$), we lift the model to multi-group multi-layer order parameters $M_{l,i}$. The resulting high-dimensional stochastic dynamics (dimension = number of attractors − 1) naturally generates 8–40 coexisting stable states at weak inter-group coupling ($\lambda \approx 0$). Strong coupling ($\lambda \gtrsim 0.3$) induces a winner-take-all collapse to 1–4 attractors, dominating over additive noise. Noise selectively erodes basins only in the critical regime ($\lambda \approx 0.3$), while weak-coupling deep wells remain robust. Heterogeneous topologies further modulate the transition: power-law degree distributions delay collapse (heterogeneity buffering), whereas high-overlap hyperedges accelerate it (excessive local competition). These findings establish a mechanistic framework for controlling multistability in competitive hypergraph systems and reconcile the apparent paradox between deterministic dimensionality and stochastic robustness.

**Keywords**: hypergraph, multistability, capacity constraint, coupling-induced collapse, noise sensitivity, topological heterogeneity

# 中文摘要

本文提出超图容量约束竞争动力学中的多稳态统一理论。从一维序参量系统（最多双稳态，非对称双势阱，临界容量 $K_c/N \approx 0.35$）出发，通过多群体多层序参量 $M_{l,i}$ 实现高维扩展。弱耦合（$\lambda \approx 0$）下自然涌现 8–40 个共存稳定态；强耦合（$\lambda \gtrsim 0.3$）驱动 winner-take-all 坍缩至 1–4 个吸引子，且主导噪声效应。噪声仅在临界区（$\lambda \approx 0.3$）显著抹平盆地，弱耦合深势阱对噪声鲁棒。异质拓扑进一步调控相变：幂律度分布推迟坍缩（异质缓冲），高重叠超边加速坍缩（局部过度竞争）。本工作为竞争超图系统的多稳态主动控制提供了机制框架，并揭示了确定性维度与随机鲁棒性之间的悖论。

**关键词**：超图、多稳态、容量约束、耦合诱导坍缩、噪声敏感、拓扑异质性
